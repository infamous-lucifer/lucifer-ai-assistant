# Scrambled Eggs

## Tags


## Ingredients
-   2 large eggs
-   1 tablespoon butter or oil
-   Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions
1. In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter or oil.
2. Beat the eggs in a bowl until they are well mixed.
3. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and stir continuously with a spatula until the eggs are scrambled to your liking.
4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
